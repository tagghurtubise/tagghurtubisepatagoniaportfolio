About

My relationship with Patagonia

  • 2010

    Park City, UT

    A mustard yellow quarter zip Nano Puff. My first Patagonia jacket at age fourteen and I still take it everywhere I go. Nine years later, my indestructible puffy sports more duct tape each year from climbing and skiing. This one jacket converted me into a lifelong customer and altered my beliefs about when and where clothing should be purchased.

  • 2013

    San Clemente, CA

    Junior year of high school, I moved from Utah to one of the last small beach towns in Southern California. The entire community revolved their schedule around tides and swells. Here, I fell in love with surfing. When a proposed toll road threatened to destroy our native wildlife and historical surf breaks, I saw first-hand how large companies can negatively impact our favorite environments.

  • 2017

    San Diego, CA

    Attending San Diego State University as a Marketing B.S. major, I knew I was finally ready to apply for the internship I wanted since I was 14. Little did I know, 9,000 other people had the same idea!

  • 2018

    San Clemente, CA

    A rejection letter last year only fueled my desire to apply to Patagonia again. I spent months perfecting myself for the next round! In the spring of 2019 I will be studying abroad and attending Paris School of Business to study Luxury Brand Management.

  • 2019!

My Skills

Why I’m a perfect fit

Online Reputation

I became an online reputation expert in my previous role at Chatmeter. I know all the tips and tricks to ensure each product has incredible online visibility. I understand the proper mix of SEO to SEM and I always value time and money in relation to the product.

Content Creation

Creation and innovation. I want to stretch the boundaries of product display and design visually appealing content. With years of practice and curiosity, I taught myself every app in Adobe Creative Suite. However, I wanted to further my skills in order to drive the conversation. I took a semester off from college and learned how to code in HTML and CSS. I even built this website using Bootstrap! These programs allow me to spark the important conversation points between the designers and the developers.

Leadership/Communication

Undoubtedly, my greatest skill is connecting with everyone I meet.  Community and communication are essential in creating an amazing product. I’ve always known, work becomes play when surrounded by like-minded employees.
