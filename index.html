About

Patagonia's Impact on Myself

  • 2010

    Park City, UT

    I was 14 when I got my first Patagonia jacket. A mustard yellow, quarter zip, Nano Puff. Although the puffy sports more duct tape each year, from climbing and skiing, I still take it everywhere I go. This is the jacket that converted me into a life long customer and changed my beliefs about how often clothing should be purchased.

  • 2013

    San Clemente, CA

    As a junior in high school, I moved from Utah to one of the last small beach towns in California. Here, I fell in love with surfing and as a result, started to see the very real consequences humans can have on the environment.

  • 2017

    San Diego, CA

    Attending San Diego State University as a Marketing B.S. major, I felt I was finally ready to apply for the internship I wanted since I was 14. Little did I know 9,000 other people had the same idea!

  • 2018

    San Clemente, CA

    Getting a rejection letter last year only fueled my desire to apply again. This time however, I knew I had to me stronger and better prepared. Because of this I have spent months perfecting myself for the next round! In the spring of 2019 I will be studying abroad and attending Paris School of Business in order to get a Luxury Brand Management Certificate.
